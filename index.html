Company

COM is a indie animation company consisting of anime industry veterans who've worked on shows like as My Hero Academia, Spy X family, Urusei Yatsura and many more.

‎

We're currently creating a project titled "I fell in love with the bunny knight", a medieval action romance show. we hope you'll keep an eye out for it!

‎

‎Currently in anime, big conglomerate companies rule over anime singlehandedly, despite generating such great profits, the animators are underpaid, mistreated, and overworked, there are so many retakes in anime that burn out overseas staff, despite alot of these retakes being easily avoidable if the studios had more foresight. such as:

‎

‎-Hiring better animation directors

‎-Directors with a clear vision of what it is that they want, instead of issuing retakes

‎-Paying their outsourced animators on time

‎-Respecting animators and industry workers

‎

‎After decades of hard work, it's time for animators to get paid fairly, without wage disparities between animators and executives, we also want to offer a chance to people who help us make anime, to share in some of the profits, so we can make this something we all support, and gain from mutually, promoting authentic handdrawn animation, this is the goal of COM.

‎

‎We thank you for your continued support of animation.
